Privacy Policy

Finnish Passphrase Generator

Effective date:

Finnish Passphrase Generator is designed to work offline and generate passphrases locally on your device.

Data we collect

We do not require accounts and we do not collect personal information for the app to function.

Purchases

If you purchase premium features, payment is processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive your payment details.

Diagnostics

Apple may provide aggregated diagnostics and crash reports to developers depending on your device settings. This helps improve stability. These reports do not include the passphrases you generate.

Passphrases & clipboard

Passphrases are generated on-device. If you use “Copy”, the text is placed on your clipboard so you can paste it elsewhere. Clipboard contents are managed by iOS.
