About the App

Finnish Passphrase Generator creates secure, memorable passphrases using Finnish words. Everything is generated locally on your device.

  • Choose number of words and max word length
  • Set capitalization and separators
  • Advanced options (premium): diacritics, avoid similar characters, replacements
  • Strength estimation using entropy (premium)

Troubleshooting / FAQ

Passphrase says “No words available”

This usually means your max word length is too small or the dictionary has not finished loading. Please wait until the loading indicator completes, or increase “Max length”.

Premium purchase does not unlock features

Try “Restore purchases”. If you are using TestFlight, premium features may already be unlocked for testing. If the issue persists, contact support.

Does the app collect any personal data?

The app is designed to work offline. Passphrases are generated locally on your device. No analytics or personal tracking is required for basic functionality.
